Welcome to Mom's Recipe Chest!

This app helps you manage your family recipes and keep track of your ingredient inventory. Here's how to get started:

  • Use the Search bar to find recipes by name, category, or ingredient.
  • Click the Add Recipe form below to input your favorite recipes.
  • Click the Edit button on any recipe to modify its details, update ingredients, or adjust instructions. Don't forget to save your changes!
  • Switch to the Inventory tab to manage your ingredients and create shopping lists.
