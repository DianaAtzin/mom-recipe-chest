Welcome to Mom's Recipe Chest!

This app helps you manage your family recipes and keep track of your ingredient inventory.

  • Use the Search bar to find recipes by name, category, or ingredient.
  • Click Add Recipe to create new recipes.
  • Click Edit on any recipe to modify its details, ingredients, or instructions. Save your changes!
  • Switch to the Inventory tab to manage ingredients and shopping lists.
  • Mark recipes as Favorites for quick access.
